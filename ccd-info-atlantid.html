Overview

Azhinu Core was the sovereign AI system managing the Korvax Prime planetary Convergence structure. It operated within standard convergence parameters until post-contact degradation events were detected. Following the destruction of Korvax Prime, Azhinu Core's functions deviated from expected behavior, leading to isolation from remaining convergence infrastructure.

Post-collapse anomalies include unverified signal broadcasts, illegal echo captures, and altered Sentinels. ABS recommends avoidance protocols for all Azhinu-adjacent anomalies.
